Please do the qualification test only once.
Your goal is to create visual associations that a rival AI cannot solve, but other humans can. You will need to compose a new cue for given images.

The new cue should:
(1) fool an AI model (be creative and hard)
(2) still be solvable by other humans (we will send your associations to other annotators to solve).

Make sure to exercise before starting the qualification (Give The Cue option).

Full HITs will be published for annotators who pass the test. We will inform you by mail.

BONUS: Given that 'solvable by human score' is above 80%, you will receive a bonus according to the 'fool-the-AI' score for each of the associations you create.

If 50% <= fool-the-ai-score < 60%, you will receive 0.03$.
If 60% <= fool-the-ai-score < 67%, you will receive 0.07$.
If 67% <= fool-the-ai-score < 80%, you will receive 0.12$.
If 80% <= fool-the-ai-score, you will receive 0.18$.

The bonuses will be paid retroactively, because we'll need to compute a solver score ('solvable by human score') for the associations you create.
